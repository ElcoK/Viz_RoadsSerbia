About This Map

📖 Methodology

This visualisation presents a multi-criteria criticality assessment of Serbia's road network under climate-related hazards (blue spots). Road segments are scored across three dimensions and combined into an overall climate criticality index:

  • Hazard Exposure (H): degree to which a segment is exposed to natural hazards
  • Travel Disruption (T): impact on national-scale connectivity if the segment is disrupted
  • Local Accessibility (A): importance of the segment for local community access
  • Climate Criticality (CC): combined score across H, T, and A indicators

Each indicator is classified into quintiles: Very Low, Low, Medium, High, Very High. Segments with a score of zero are classified as No criticality.

🗺 How to Use

  • Use the layer panel (top right) to switch between the four criticality indicators
  • Toggle the Road Network background layer on/off
  • Toggle Road Categories to see the network coloured by road type
  • Click any road segment to see its full attribute table
  • The legend (bottom right) updates with the active layer
